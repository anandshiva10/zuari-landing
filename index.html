Our Verticals

Specialized expertise across diverse sectors with strategic focus on high-growth markets and essential commodities in India and globally.

Real Estate Trading

Commercial and residential property investments, development projects, and real estate trading with focus on emerging markets in Mumbai, Delhi, and high-yield opportunities across India.

Energy Sector Trading

Oil, gas, renewable energy, and power trading with comprehensive market analysis and risk management across global energy markets and India's growing energy sector.
